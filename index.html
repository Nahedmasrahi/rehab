About Me

I’m Dr.Rehab, a Saudi physician holding the Saudi Board in Obstetrics and Gynecology. I am deeply passionate about enhancing the quality of healthcare services and ensuring the safety of mothers.

Throughout my journey, I’ve led several impactful initiatives across the region and was honored by the Minister of Health for applying best practices in safe childbirth.

My aspiration is to continue my academic and professional path in the field of infertility and IVF through a certified international fellowship, allowing me to provide advanced and holistic care for women and children.
